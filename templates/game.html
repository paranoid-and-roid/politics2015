See if you can remember the details from the list of politicians. Simply click the "Start" button below,and you have 60 seconds to identify the correct constituency and political party for each of the names and faces you see.

The game is designed to test both your knowledge and your speed. You will receive a score equal to half the remaining seconds for correctly identifying the TD's constituency, and half for correctly choosing the TD's political party. The points available decrease as the seconds count down.
